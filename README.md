# IBM HR Analytics Dashboard 📊

An end-to-end Data Analytics project developed during my **Data Analysis Training Program** at **Orange Digital Center (ODC)**.

This project analyzes employee attrition using the IBM HR Analytics dataset and presents interactive visualizations that help identify the key factors influencing employee turnover. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## 📷 Dashboard Preview

> *A preview of the dashboard is available in the `Dashboard` folder.*

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition and workforce trends.
- Identify the main factors affecting employee turnover.
- Build an interactive dashboard for HR decision-makers.
- Generate business insights and actionable recommendations.
- Practice the complete data analytics workflow from raw data to reporting.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Microsoft Excel** – Initial data exploration.
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard development.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Custom measures and KPIs.
- **LaTeX** – Professional project report formatting.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

- KPI Cards
  - Total Employees
  - Attrition Count
  - Average Job Satisfaction
  - Average Years at Company

- Interactive Visualizations
  - Attrition Rate Gauge
  - Attrition by Department
  - Attrition by Job Role
  - Attrition by Overtime
  - Attrition by Business Travel
  - Employee Age Analysis
  - Job Satisfaction Analysis

- Interactive Slicers
  - Department
  - Job Role
  - Age
  - Overtime

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Employees who work overtime are significantly more likely to leave the company.
- Lower job satisfaction is strongly associated with higher attrition.
- Certain job roles experience higher turnover than others.
- Younger employees show a relatively higher attrition rate.
- The dashboard helps HR teams identify high-risk employee groups and supports strategic retention planning.
